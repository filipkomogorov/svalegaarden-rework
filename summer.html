Summer Menu

/June, July, August/

In this menu item you will find our large selection of gastronomic taste experiences, which are composed based on the best ingredients of the seasons.

Three dishes from NOK 400. The price will vary in relation to the raw material price.

Starters

Bag crab stirred with mild aioli and fresh herbs - served with marinated cucumber, radish, light croutons, dill and dill vinaigrette with cream double

Steamed red tongue flavored with truffle - served cold with cauliflower purée, lemon, light croutons as well as browned butter with cashews

Ibérico ham - served with sun-ripened tomatoes, black olives, fresh basil and creamy mozzerella

Main Courses

Pink roasted summer goat with new peas a la Francaise, summer cabbage and pickled onions. A part is served potatoes with fresh thyme and game sauce

Cracked breast of poussin and confit of thighs with summer green, new parsley potatoes and creamy anise broth

Fried veal tenderloin served with fresh carrots and chives, warm salad of raw marinated courgettes, cabbage and tomato. Served with veal cloud and mashed potato

Desserts

Weck Glass with white chocolate, fresh berries of the season, mint, pistachio and raspberry sorbet

White chocolate pie with fresh strawberries - served with a siphon of raspberries, crumble and homemade vanilla ice cream

Panna Cotta with soft caramel, fresh raspberries, roasted almonds and broth of elderflower and verbena
